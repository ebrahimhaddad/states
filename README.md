A dynamic, interactive web application designed for strategic business decision-making. This tool visualizes the operational territories of pharmaceutical distribution companies across the United States, helping a German pharmaceutical and medical equipment manufacturer analyze market entry strategies.

<img width="1121" height="628" alt="screenshot" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ce34e3f2-5159-400e-8ba1-6535ef6e4fb8" />

Business Context:
This project was developed for the management team of a German pharmaceutical and medical equipment company (Erotz) to support their strategic planning and US market entry analysis. The interactive visualization allows executives to quickly understand which distributors are active in which states, enabling data-driven decisions about partnerships, market gaps, and competitive landscape.

Data Layer:
The business intelligence powering this application was meticulously gathered and validated by Z-Mosayebi, a domain expert in pharmaceutical distribution. The data is structured in a JavaScript array containing: Company names & Lists of US states where each company operates

Interaction Flow:
1. User selects a company name from the radio button list on the left panel
2. JavaScript event listener detects the selection
3. The application references the data array to identify active states for that company
4. Visual feedback is applied to the corresponding state images on the map
5. Previously highlighted states are reset to maintain clarity
